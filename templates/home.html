Sanctuary of Christ the King-Portugal

  • Admin
  • 36 Comments

Perched on the banks of the Tagus River, the Sanctuary of Christ the King in Portugal stands as an iconic symbol of faith and spirituality. Towering over Lisbon, this colossal statue of Christ welcomes visitors with open arms, offering panoramic views of the city and the river. Built in 1959, it mirrors the Christ the Redeemer statue in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and serves as a place of pilgrimage and reflection. The sanctuary's serene ambiance and breathtaking vistas make it a sacred destination, inviting contemplation and connection in the heart of Portugal.
